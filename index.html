What is Tit Tar Bone Setting Therapy?

跌打 — literally “fall and strike.” A Chinese healing tradition built over centuries for exactly the injuries life gives you: the fall, the twist, the years hunched over a desk.

The diagnosis happens through the hands. We feel where the bone has shifted, where the muscle has locked to protect it, and where the pain you feel is only the echo of a problem sitting somewhere else entirely. Then it is set right — with technique, not force.

Where it helps, we bring in osteopathic and chiropractic care techniques too. We are not precious about tradition for its own sake. We are only interested in you walking out straighter than you walked in.

  • 01

    Read

    A full assessment by hand. Where the pain is is rarely where the pain starts.

  • 02

    Release

    Locked muscle and fascia are worked open first. You cannot set a bone the body is guarding.

  • 03

    Realign

    The adjustment itself. Explained to you before it happens — no surprises, no fear.

  • 04

    Retain

    What to do at home so it holds. A fix that does not last is not a fix.
